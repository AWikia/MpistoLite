What is MpistoLite?
MpistoLite is a lightweight version of Mpisto War, limiting to standards that are offered by Opera 11.50 and also being a database for wikis and can read raw text from articles. Buttons are converted into standard design and offering some fake JS to turn into anchors and dropdowns and banners are striped out. Shadow and Stroke markup are also converted into regular text.
